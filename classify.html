Classify Your Collateral

Article 9 of the UCC has different rules for different types of collateral. That's why it's very important to figure out exactly what your collateral is defined to be according to Article 9.

Which of these does your collateral sound most like?

It's a physical thing that I can move.

Can you touch it? Can you move it without causing serious damage? If you answered yes to either or both of these questions, your collateral probably falls into the category of Goods
